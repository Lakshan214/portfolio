Introduction

The software requirements specification is submitted to meet requirements of the group project module conducted by the Sri Lanka Institute Of Advanced Technological Education (SLIATE). The following system provides an overview of the online products ordering web application for grocery. The purpose of the document is presented and its intended audience outlined and the scope of the project specified by the document is given with a particular focus on what the web application will do

The project scope entails developing a comprehensive "BeautyHub Online Shopping System" within the proposed timeframe. The system will cater to four main user roles: customers, administrators, super administrators, and delivery personnel. Customers will have access to a wide range of cosmetic products, detailed product descriptions, and reviews. They can register an account, manage their profile, add items to their cart, and track their orders. Various payment options will be available to ensure a convenient and secure checkout process.

#2. Scope of the project

Administrators will oversee the platform, managing product catalogs, handling customer orders, generating invoices, and monitoring sales and profits. Super administrators will have additional privileges to manage administrators, review platform performance, and analyze business metrics to drive strategic decision-making. Delivery personnel will utilize the system to view assigned orders, update order statuses, and communicate with administrators regarding delivery logistics

Security

Each user must log into the system, using their unique name, email address, phone number, and national identity card. No one can log into the system without this data. When the user logs in after providing this data, the data is stored in the system. Users can change the data again if they want. Users must provide true information. Otherwise, the registration will not be successful. No one can access to this data. Data will be protected until and after service is provided. This system is expected to protect user data in an advanced manner.
